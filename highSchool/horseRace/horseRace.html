Endurance Racing

Instructions for Endurance Racing

The Goal of the game is to reach the end of the course as fast as possible.
Use the ‘A’ and ‘D’ keys to move side to side, and the 'W' and 'S' keys
to move backwards and forwards, to avoid the following:

  • Horses
  • Branches
  • Rocks
  • Puddles
  • Logs

The longer you last without hitting an object, the faster you go.
Beware, if you hit an object, your speed will be reduced to what you started at.
